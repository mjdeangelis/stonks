How are share prices determined?

The Commission will use weekly power rankings provided by NFL.com. 33-(power rank) = Share Price. (eg. Eagles are 3rd in power rankings: 33-3 = $30/share

How do I purchase stock?

You have until Thursday 12am of NFL Week 1 to purchase stock from the exchange. You can continue to purchase stocks from the exchange throughout the season for stocks not owned by anyone else in the marketplace. If stock is owned by another trader, you must buy and sell amongst yourselves.
